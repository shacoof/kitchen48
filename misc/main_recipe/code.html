Garlic Herb Pasta

A quick, aromatic Mediterranean-inspired dish that brings gourmet flavors to your table in minutes. Perfectly balanced with fresh herbs and toasted garlic.

timer

15m

30m

Servings

2-4

Ingredients

Linguine Pasta 250g
Garlic Cloves 6 large
Extra Virgin Olive Oil 1/4 cup
Fresh Parsley 1 bunch
Red Chili Flakes 1 tsp
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup
format_list_numbered

Cooking Steps

1

Boil the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Cook the linguine until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.

2

Infuse the Oil

While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add thinly sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© until garlic is golden and fragrant, but not burnt.

3

Emulsify the Sauce

Add the cooked pasta and reserved pasta water to the skillet. Toss vigorously over medium heat until the water and oil create a glossy sauce that coats every strand.

4

Finish & Garnish

Remove from heat. Stir in fresh parsley and grated parmesan. Season with black pepper and serve immediately while steaming hot.
